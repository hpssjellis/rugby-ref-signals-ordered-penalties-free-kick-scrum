Trying to get a sense of the most often called rugby infringements with there ref signals

See my website at https://www.rocksetta.com/rugbyref/

After 30 years of rugby coaching I have started reffing and am having a hard time learning the ref secondary signals. He is an attempt to order them by most often called. (Please send a pull request or message me on twitter @rocksetta if you wish to make an addition or suggest a change in the order )

Ref Signals in order of Most Often Called (Very un-scientific just how I feel about it.)

  1. Penalty (Primary Ref Signal)

  2. Scrum (Primary Ref Signal)

  3. Knock-on (scrum)

  4. Throw forward or forward pass (scrum)

  5. Try

  6. Tackler or tackled player not rolling away (penalty)

  7. Not releasing ball immediately in the tackle (penalty)

  8. Tackler not releasing tackled player (penalty)

  9. Handling ball in ruck or scrum (penalty)

  10. Offside at scrum, ruck or maul (penalty)

  11. Throw at lineout not straight (scrum)

  12. Forming a scrum

  13. High tackle (foul play) (penalty)

  14. Time off

  15. Timekeeper to stop and start watch

  16. Offside under 10-metre law or not 10 metres at penalty and free-kicks (penalty)

  17. Obstruction in open play (penalty)

  18. Failure to bind (penalty)

  19. Offside in the lineout (penalty)

  20. Offside choice: penalty or scrum

  21. Diving to the ground near the tackle (penalty)

  22. Intentionally collapsing ruck or maul (penalty)

  23. 22 drop-out

  24. Prop pulling down opponent (penalty)

  25. Referee consults TMO

  26. Entering tackle from wrong direction (penalty)

  27. Ball held up in in-goal (scrum)

  28. Unplayable ball in ruck or tackle (scrum)

  29. Unplayable ball in maul (scrum)

  30. Scrum wheeled more than 90 degrees (scrum)

  31. Free Kick (Primary Ref Signal)

  32. Foot-up by front-row player (free-kick)

  33. Throw at scrum not straight (free-kick)

  34. Closing gaps in lineout (free-kick)

  35. Early lifting and lifting in lineout (free-kick)

  36. Head injury assessment required

  38. Intentionally falling over on a player (penalty)

  39. Joining a ruck or a maul in front of the back foot and from the side (penalty)

  40. Doctor needed

  41. Bleeding wound

  42. Prop pulling opponent (penalty)

  43. Barging in lineout (penalty)

  44. Leaning on player in lineout (penalty)

  45. Pushing opponent in lineout (penalty)

  46. Stamping (foul play) (penalty)

  47. Punching (foul play) (penalty)

  48. Physiotherapist needed
